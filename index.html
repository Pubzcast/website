Synopsis

Sorry I’m late! Been talking about talking for ages, it's time I hit the record/stream button and finally share some of the ideas that I’ve become committed to over time. These ideas, which I refer to as recurring topics, are observations that come back to hit me in the face, no matter how hard I try to forget, oh and I try.

I will use the pubzcast to argue a perspective we rarely, if ever hear in the mainstream press, that of citizen, regardless of sex, gender, ethnicity, etc. Instead of assuming the rights and responsibilities that go with civic participation in a healthy democracy, we have been reduced to passive consumers or left in a state of perpetual adolescence.

In the last decades, we have seen a movement towards an increased privatization in all areas, devastating the public sectors including health, education, transportation, etc. Add identitarianism to the mix, and you have an already fragmented population divided even further. Is this all coincidence or is this all by design? What do you think? Let’s explore these ideas together!

My training is in the social sciences and my politics are left-of-center. I have strong environmental values with a focus on the long term sustainability of the planet. Watch the video where I take the political compass quiz.

Pubzcast will be covering a variety of topics from news, current events, history, public policy and politics. The show will be a work in progress as I play with different formats and ideas. For example:

  • Sharing my Worldview
  • Call-in shows (via Discord, Skype, etc)
  • Outline of Season 1
  • News Headlines (5 or 10 minute segments)
  • Documentary Reviews
  • Omegle/Monkey.app
  • Gaming Streams using gaming accessories such as a racing wheel or arcade joystick.
  • Misc: Play with AI art, Fight Companions for UFC PPV events with possible pre and post fight analysis.

Here to put my ideas to the test, for there is nothing better to set a man straight than to put his ideas out there to be scrutinized and analysed by the collective power of the internet.
